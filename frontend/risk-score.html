📖 Risk Score Methodology
🟢 Low Risk (0–29)

Strong audit trail, high utilization, minimal irregularities. Routine monitoring sufficient.

🟡 Medium Risk (30–54)

Some irregularities detected. Enhanced monitoring and quarterly audits recommended.

🔴 High Risk (55–74)

Significant anomalies present. Immediate investigation and disbursement review required.

🟣 Critical (75–100)

Fraud suspected. Escalate to CBI/CAG immediately. Consider freezing disbursements.
